About Astra


During the third semester of pursuing B. Tech at Punjab Engineering College, we faced a problem
of not knowing enough about the projects that we were supposed to make for improving our resumes
and being prepared for the internship and placement oppurtunities provided by several companies.

Besides that, we had little knowledge about the interview procedures of these companies. It was
then that we came up with the idea of Astra, a platform where the students of PEC can find
information about all these things.
